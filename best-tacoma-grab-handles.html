Why Not Just Use the Stock Handle?

The stock Tacoma grab handle is fundamentally a piece of curved plastic with a thin rubber coating. It's designed for aesthetics, not load-bearing. When you're loaded down with gear and trying to pull yourself into the cab, the stock handle flexes and can actually bend further over time. That's not an issue with a 50-pound pull — but if you're mounting a rooftop tent or pulling yourself up with a heavy pack, it's a liability.

Bartact's paracord design has been widely regarded as a stronger premium option than generic strap-style handles. The hardware is stainless steel and the cord itself is rated for hundreds of pounds of static load.

What About MOLLE Grab Handles?

Some grab handles add MOLLE-compatible loops on the back so you can strap them to MOLLE panels on seat covers. Bartact's Tacomagrab handles work alongside their seat covers' MOLLE webbing for a fully integrated system.
