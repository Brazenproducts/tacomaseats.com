Best Tacoma Interior Accessories

April 2026 · 7 min read

You’ve got your seat covers sorted. Now let’s complete the interior. These accessories pair perfectly with seat covers to protect and organize your Tacoma’s cabin.

Floor Mats

Best Overall

WeatherTech FloorLiners

WeatherTech makes the most precise-fit floor liners on the market. Their Tacoma-specific liners cover the entire footwell floor with raised edges that contain water, mud, and debris. They’re easy to pull out and hose off. The digital-fit measurement means they follow every contour of your Tacoma’s floor. Front + rear set runs $150-200.

Check Amazon Price

Best Value

Husky Liners X-act Contour

Husky Liners are the main WeatherTech alternative, and honestly, they’re about 90% as good at 70% of the price. The rubberized material is slightly softer (which some people prefer), and the fit is excellent for the Tacoma. If you don’t want to pay WeatherTech prices, Husky is the answer. $120-160 for front + rear.

Check Amazon Price

Center Console Organizer

Vehicle OCD Center Console Organizer

The Tacoma’s center console is basically one big bin that swallows everything into a jumbled mess. Vehicle OCD makes a drop-in organizer with compartments for phone, wallet, sunglasses, coins, and small tools. It’s the most satisfying $30 you’ll spend on your truck.

MOLLE Panel & Storage

If you went with Bartact tactical covers, you’ve got MOLLE webbing on your seatbacks. Here’s what to attach to it:

Tactical MOLLE Pouches

Generic MOLLE pouches from brands like Condor and Vism work great on Bartact seatbacks. A small admin pouch for documents, a medical pouch for a basic IFAK, and a water bottle holder give you trail-ready organization without cluttering the cabin.

Dash & Visor Accessories

Tacoma Dash Mat

The Tacoma dashboard reflects badly off the windshield, especially in the afternoon sun. A custom dash mat eliminates the glare and protects the dash from UV cracking. DashMat on Amazon — $30-50 and custom-fit to your Tacoma.

Shop at Bartact →

Cargo / Bed Accessories

While these aren’t strictly interior, Tacoma owners upgrading their interior usually care about the bed too:

  • Bed mat: BedRug or rubber bed mats protect the bed from scratches and keep cargo from sliding. $60-150.
  • Bed organizer: DECKED drawer system turns your bed into organized storage. $1,200+ but incredibly useful.
  • Tonneau cover: Protects your gear from weather and theft. RealTruck has the best selection for Tacomas.
